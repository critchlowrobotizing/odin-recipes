Marinara Sauce Recipe

Ingredients

    Based on feeding a hostel of 4 Friends
  • Fresh Tomatoes - 6-8 medium sized
  • Garlic - 6-8 cloves
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper
  • Any good, edible mushroom
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1-2 large red onion
  • 1 large Bell Pepper
  • Basil (a good handful at least)
  • 1 Zucchini
  • Pasta (Angel hair is my favorite)
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Make sure everything is prepped, cut the veggies and have things staged a bit when the cooking starts to move fast
  2. Heat up the pan with olive oil and once hot, add the ground beef. You can sautee some garlic initially too if you want to flavor the meat a bit.
  3. Once the meat is browned, remove the beef from the heat and add the rest of your garlic.
  4. Sautee the garlic for a few seconds in the meat juice and oil, then add the onion and bell pepper
  5. Soften the onion and pepper, add some seasoning and salt and pepper.
  6. Add the Zucchini and Mushrooms and some additional seasoning. We want to season throughout the process so each of the ingredients can absorb the seasony goodness.
  7. Our final step will be to add the tomatoes after the mushrooms and Zucchini have softened a bit. The tomatoes will need to cook for a bit and cook off some of the water. Keep an eye out for the sauce as you stir and begin to mix everything together.
  8. Final final step will be to cook the pasta and add the ground beef back in. Make sure you boil the water in super salty water so its real hot and once you have cooked the pasta, flash it in cold water to keep it from cooking. Then add the ground beef back into the sauce and allow as much time on low heat as possible. I usually start this recipe at 3pm so the flavor can absorb for a couple hours after I am done.
