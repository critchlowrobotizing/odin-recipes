Healthy Breakfast Oats

Breakfast is the most important meal of the day and this recipe is one of the most healthy recipes you can put in your body. You can even make it as an overnight oats recipe if you want to meal prep and overachieve. My hat is off to those that are this responsible.

Ingredients

    Made for one person
  • Oats, Unflavored, plain, classic oats
  • Plain yogurt (NO ADDED SUGAR)
  • Blueberries
  • Cinnamon
  • Chia Seeds
  • Almonds
  • Walnuts
  • Flax Seeds
  • Local Honey
  • Penut Butter (Optional)

Steps

  1. Cook your oats either on stove or in microwave
  2. Add everything in. Quantities are fairly straightforwared, just add what you want in the quanitities you want. It is all good for you so customize it yourself!
  3. Enjoy!
