Healthy Breakfast Eggs

Knock out your daily needs of veggies and protein with eggs packed with everything that I consider delicious. Cooking eggs can be done in a variety of ways so how exactly you will cook this will change and improve based on how you like your eggs.

Ingredients

    Based on feeding 2 Friends
  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper
  • Any good, edible mushroom
  • Chorizo
  • 1 large red onion
  • 1 large Bell Pepper
  • herbs to season
  • Avocado (optional)
  • Bread or Tortilla
  • Jalepeno (optional)
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make sure everything is prepped, cut the veggies and have things staged a bit when the cooking starts to move fast
  2. Start with the chorizo, cook this down a bit until it is fully cooked. Then sautee the garlic for a few seconds in the meat juice and oil, then add the onion and bell pepper
  3. Soften the onion and pepper, add some seasoning and salt and pepper.
  4. Add the mushrooms and some additional seasoning. We want to season throughout the process so each of the ingredients can absorb the seasony goodness.
  5. Then add the tomatoes after the mushrooms have softened a bit. The tomatoes will need to cook for a bit and cook off some of the water. Keep an eye out for the sauce as you stir and begin to mix everything together.
  6. Final step will be to add the eggs. Crack your choice of eggs in and cover with a lid. Be very mindful here as it is easy to over cook your eggs. I always serve with a carb of your choice: toasted bread, tortilla, or hashbrowns.
